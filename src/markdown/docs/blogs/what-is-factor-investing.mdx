## Common Equity Factors

- Value: cheap vs. expensive securities
- Momentum: recent winners continue to outperform
- Quality: profitability and balance-sheet strength
- Size: small vs. large capitalization
- Low volatility: lower-risk stocks often outperform on a risk-adjusted basis

## Implementation Notes

Neutralize undesired exposures, control turnover, and account for transaction costs.
