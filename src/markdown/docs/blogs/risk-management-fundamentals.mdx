## Why Risk Management?

Survivability matters. Risk frameworks limit losses, stabilize returns, and support compounding.

## Core Concepts

- Volatility and drawdowns
- Position sizing and leverage
- Stop-losses and exposure caps
- Risk parity and diversification

## Practical Tips

Stress test scenarios, track realized vs. forecast risk, and keep assumptions explicit.
