## Overview

Stochastic volatility (SV) models treat volatility as a random process, capturing features like volatility clustering and mean reversion.

## Popular Models

- Heston model (square-root diffusion)
- SABR (stochastic alpha, beta, rho)
- GARCH family (discrete-time analogues)

## Applications

Derivatives pricing, risk forecasting, and realistic scenario generation for stress testing.
